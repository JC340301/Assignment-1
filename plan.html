Goals

This webpage also aims to advertise CossiePlay and its monthly cosplay parties to increase visitation; more specifically to penetrate into a younger market segment since the internet is dominated by the younger population. 1. The number of visitors of the webpage every week.
2. The increase in visitation of the monthly parties after establishment of the webpage.
3. The increase in demand for the company's products after establishment of the webpage.
4. The increase in revenue following the establishment of the webpage.

Target Audience

The main target audience are for high school students and young adults.
